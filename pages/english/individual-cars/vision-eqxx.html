Vision - EQXX

The Vision-EQXX is a highly anticipated concept car from Mercedes-Benz that is set to debut in 2023. It is an electric vehicle that aims to push the boundaries of what is possible in terms of electric range and efficiency.

We estimate that that the Vision-EQXX will have a range of over 1,000 kilometers (620 miles) on a single charge, which would be a significant improvement over most electric vehicles currently on the market. The car is also expected to feature a highly efficient drivetrain, with advanced battery technology and regenerative braking.

In terms of design, the Vision-EQXX is expected to be sleek and futuristic, with a focus on aerodynamics and efficiency. It will likely feature advanced technology features such as a large touchscreen infotainment system, digital instrument cluster, and advanced driver assistance systems.

As an AMG model, the Vision-EQXX is also expected to have impressive performance, with precise handling and acceleration. It remains to be seen exactly what the final specs and features of the Vision-EQXX will be, but it is expected to be a significant step forward for electric vehicles and a showcase of Mercedes-Benz's advanced engineering capabilities.
