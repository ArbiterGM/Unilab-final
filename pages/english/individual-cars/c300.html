C300 - AMG

The 2023 Mercedes-Benz C300 AMG is a performance-oriented version of the C-Class sedan. It features a turbocharged 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine that produces 255 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, which is paired with a nine-speed automatic transmission and rear-wheel drive as standard. All-wheel drive is available as an option.

The C300 AMG also comes with a sport-tuned suspension, larger brakes, and other performance upgrades that make it more agile and fun to drive. The exterior of the C300 AMG features more aggressive front and rear bumpers, a rear spoiler, and AMG-specific wheels.

Inside, the C300 AMG has sport seats with adjustable side bolsters, an AMG steering wheel, and a unique gauge cluster. It also features a larger infotainment screen and a digital instrument cluster as standard.

Overall, the 2023 Mercedes-Benz C300 AMG is a great option for those looking for a luxurious and sporty sedan that offers impressive performance and handling capabilities.
