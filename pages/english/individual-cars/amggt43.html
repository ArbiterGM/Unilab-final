AMG - GT43

A four-door coupe that combines sporty driving dynamics with luxurious comfort and advanced technology. It's part of the AMG GT family, which is known for its high-performance sports cars.

Under the hood, the GT43 features a turbocharged 3.0-liter inline-six engine that produces 362 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. It's paired with a nine-speed automatic transmission and rear-wheel drive as standard, all-wheel drive is also an option. The GT43 can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in around 4.8 seconds and has a top speed of 168 mph.

The exterior of the GT43 is sleek and sporty, short overhangs, and a sloping roofline that gives it a coupe-like profile. It also features a distinctive AMG front grille and a rear diffuser. The GT43 rides on 19-inch wheels as standard, with 20-inch wheels available as an option.

Inside, the GT43 offers a luxurious and high-tech cabin. It features a large digital instrument cluster and infotainment screen, a sporty steering wheel with touch-sensitive controls, and premium materials such as leather upholstery and real wood trim. The rear seats can accommodate two adults, making the GT43 a practical choice for those who need more space than a traditional sports car.

Overall, the Mercedes-AMG GT43 is a great option for those who want the performance and style of a sports car with the practicality and comfort of a four-door sedan.
